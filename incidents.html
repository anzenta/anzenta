Incidents/Accidents


Accident/incident investigation or analysis provides essential information for both management and employees. It means that in possession of gathered information, future accidents can be prevented. By finding the root cause and other detected facts your company can take corrective actions in order to prevent or reduce the number of accidents.


With Anzenta consultants’ practical experiences, we are capable of leading the investigation to specify appropriate corrective actions and ascertain root causes, so future accident/incidents can be controlled or eliminated.



Every incident that leads to personal injuries, near misses or property damages, needs to be investigated. All facts or revealed findings provide a perfect possibility to improve your safety management system or raise workers’ safety awareness. We desire to find the real facts and causes, not to put the blame on anybody.

In case you need an independent investigation team, an investigator or an investigation team leader, we provide you with our professional services. Our investigation team will ensure that the relevant protocols for gathering, handling and storing evidences with confidence.


Upon utilizing our investigation services, your company will earn the following benefits:

• asset investigation can tell you a lot about how things are going in real life,
• it helps you uncover and correct any breaches in legal compliance you may have been unaware of,
• it reveals the gaps in your safety system, before people are harmed,
• it will help demonstrate a positive health and safety attitude and management commitment,
• findings provide essential information for insurers, reduce the likelihood of fraudulent claims.


And your staff will acquire the following advantages:

• increases workers ’self-confidence and eliminates their fears of possible injury,
• increases safety culture and promotes better morale among all workers,
• measures reduce the negative financial impact.
