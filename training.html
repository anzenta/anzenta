Training


Workplace safety training is an essential and critical part of a successful safety management system. In general, the workers must be up to date with the risks affecting them on a daily basis A well-managed workplace safety training program can be the most useful tool in educating employees and ensuring that they learn the latest safety practice and are motivated to always work safely. Anzenta offers a wide range of safety training from basic legal compliance to client tailored on-line solutions.


Workers with EHS knowledge and culture are more empowered and confident about the Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) skills. The main benefit is that employees become more engaged in improving the overall level of workplace safety. Our approach is to achieve an encouraged positive safety culture in our client’s workplaces. We aim to develop the workers’ observational skills, improve their hazard recognition and ability to create and maintain a safe work environment. Anzenta consultants provide a wide range of safety training from basic legal compliance to client tailored on-line solutions. We would like to know the intention of our clients and assess the details of their workplace.



With these experiences we utilize effective delivery methods:

• on-site workshops,
• webinars,
• e-Learning modules,
• combined training solutions.


Without striving for completeness, we would like to be your partner on the following training topics:
• General regulatory compliance in occupational, -fire and -environmental safety,
• Safety Induction,
• Health & Safety Leadership,
• Permit to Work and Confined Space Operations,
• Environmental Protection and Waste Management,
• First Aid Training,
• Working at Height,
• Chemical Handling,
• Lock out Tag out,
• Construction- and Excavation Safety,
• Hot Works Safety,
• Emergency Response.



To keep physical distance workers are encouraged or obliged to work from home if the nature of their job allows it. Working environment at home is likely to be deficient in many aspects compared to company workplace. Anzenta can advise you on how to stay safe and healthy while working from home.
