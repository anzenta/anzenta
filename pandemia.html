Pandemia


The COVID-19 pandemic has impacted every part of our society and our economy, also put pressure on employers and workers to implement new HSE procedures and practices in a very short time.


Anzenta is prepared to help you in developing COVID-19 Response Plan. We are familiar with the most up-to-date official public health advice and guidance of COVID-19.



Most of the workplaces have been closed or re-organized for a period for reasons related to COVID-19. In advance of returning to work, employers are obliged to make plans and take actions such as:

• employers have a duty to reduce workplace risk to the lowest reasonably practicable level by taking preventative measures. We offer to review and update your occupational health and safety risk assessment,
• pay special attention to workers who are at high risk,
• consider workers who may be suffering from anxiety or stress,
• consider contingency measures to address increased rates of worker absenteeism,
• implement of the measures necessary to reduce the transmission of COVID-19,
• communicate with workers to avoid the spread of COVID-19 in the workplace,
