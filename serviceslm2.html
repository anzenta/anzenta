EHS auditing, compliance

We offer customized health and safety and environment (HSE) auditing services by our experienced auditors. Or if you have your own auditing staff, we are happy to prepare you an HSE Legal register, and audit checklists.  Audits are an important tool for assuring compliance with  Regulatory requirements  Management Systems  Internal Policies & Standards In general, an HSE audit consists of employee interviews, detailed record reviews, and site visits. Based on this information, we determine non-compliances and suggest corrective actions and recommend the best management practices. The objective is to improve your EHS performance, prevent violations and fines, and demonstrate due diligence. Be proactive. Do not wait until the potential problems happen.
